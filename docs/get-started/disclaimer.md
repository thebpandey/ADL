# Disclaimer and risk acknowledgment

*Disclaimer version 1 — this is the text the [Get Started wizard](/get-started)
asks you to acknowledge before showing personalized executable instructions.
Browsing the knowledge base never requires acceptance.*

assessments and installation instructions are provided for informational
purposes and are not guarantees. Android devices, operating-system updates,
manufacturer security controls, applications, Linux packages, and peripheral
combinations can behave differently or change without notice.

Following these instructions may require installing applications outside an
app store, changing security or battery settings, using developer options,
running terminal commands, or installing large amounts of software. This can
result in data loss, reduced security, instability, excessive battery use,
heat, poor performance, application incompatibility, interrupted updates, or
other unexpected behavior.

Back up important data before proceeding. Review every command and script.
Restore security settings when instructed. You are responsible for deciding
whether to continue and for the results on your device.

## What acknowledgment means in the wizard

Before showing your personalized guide, the wizard asks you to confirm five
separate statements (none pre-checked):

1. I understand compatibility is not guaranteed.
2. I understand instructions may change security or battery settings.
3. I have backed up important data, or accept the risk of proceeding
   without a backup.
4. I will review commands and scripts before running them.
5. I accept responsibility for changes made to my device.

Your acknowledgment (version and timestamp) is stored **only in your
browser** — it is never transmitted anywhere. Exported plans record which
disclaimer version was acknowledged. If this disclaimer materially changes,
the wizard asks again.
